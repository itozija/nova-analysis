# Nova Analysis

A personal intelligence system built on ZeroClaw.

## What this is

Nova Analysis is a toolkit that turns your AI agent into a working intelligence system:

- **Nova** — monitors YouTube 24/7 and converts videos into business intelligence briefs
- **Second Brain T** — turns any folder of documents into a searchable knowledge base
- **Telegram** — command both from your phone, anywhere

## Who this is for

Anyone who wants to own AI infrastructure, not just use AI tools.

## What you get

- A YouTube analyst that runs on your VPS and reports to your Telegram
- A knowledge base built from your own documents (PDFs, notes, research)
- One command to query everything from Telegram
