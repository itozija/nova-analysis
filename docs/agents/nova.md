# Nova — YouTube Intelligence Agent

Nova is a Content Intelligence Officer that converts YouTube videos into business intelligence briefs.

## What Nova does

1. Receives a YouTube URL via Telegram
2. Scrapes the transcript and metadata
3. Outputs a structured brief — under 300 words

## Output format

Every brief starts with an ROI verdict, then bullet points only:

    HIGH ROI
    - Founder bootstrapped to $4M ARR with zero paid marketing
    - Core mechanic: weekly email digest monetised via sponsorships
    - Replicable model: audience first, product second
    - Key insight: niche authority compounds faster than broad reach
